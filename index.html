Hi.

I'm a developer, designer, and photographer based in Providence, Rhode Island.

I believe that technology has an incredible power to make the world better. I see it as my job to create tools that improve the lives of others.

I'm a student at Brown University, where I study computer science and math, in addition to creative writing, political science, and cognitive science.

I became a developer because I love watching thousands of lines of code on a screen turn into a real-world product. I started out with Python, then moved to iOS development with Objective-C, before arriving at full-stack web development. Today, I spend my time building web applications using frameworks like Angular, Express, Node, and Bootstrap. I also mess around with design work, using tools like Photoshop and Illustrator.

I've been part of two startups, and will be interning at MongoDB this summer.

In addition to writing code, I teach classes on coding and math at a local private school, online, and for afterschool enrichment programs. I love good food (especially coffee), ultimate frisbee, reading, discussing politics, and gaming. Oh, and I can juggle.
