I'm Ben Murphy.

I'm a freshman at Brown University in Providence, Rhode Island, studying applied math and computer science.
I'm also a photographer, developer, writer, and ultimate frisbee player.

I enjoy developing for mobile and web, and I'm proficient in Objective-C, Python, and HTML/CSS. My business experience includes two startup companies, one of which I co-founded, the other of which I interned at for a year. At the moment, I work teaching math and computer science at The Wheeler School, and teaching math as part of the Art of Problem Solving.

Other interests of mine include politics, competition math, and gaming.
